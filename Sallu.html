The Story Behind Saloni's Creation

What began as a small passion for resin art has grown into a heartfelt studio dedicated to creating one-of-a-kind pieces for every occasion.

Each item is hand-poured, cured, and finished with care — meaning no two pieces are ever exactly alike. When you order from Saloni's Creation, you're getting art made just for you.

We specialize in custom colors, personalized names, and festival-themed designs — because every celebration deserves something special.
